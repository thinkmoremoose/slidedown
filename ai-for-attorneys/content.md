# From AI Fundamentals to Operational Excellence

**A Technical Literacy Roadmap for Legal Professionals**

Speaker: David "Moose" Wolpoff — Co-founder & CTO, Sidekick Labs

---

## Speaker Bio

David "moose" Wolpoff is the co-founder and CTO of Sidekick Labs (an
AI-native cybersecurity platform). A veteran of the cybersecurity industry,
David has spent his career in the trenches of offensive security,
vulnerability research, and embedded systems design. He was previously the
co-founder and CTO of Randori through its 2022 acquisition by IBM. David is
a graduate of the University of Colorado, holding both a Bachelor's and
Master's in Electrical Engineering.
