## What Do We Actually Need?
<p>Strip it down to first principles. A presentation needs to:</p>
* <span class="green">✓</span> Display content on a screen[[fragment]]
* <span class="green">✓</span> Navigate between pages[[fragment]]
* <span class="green">✓</span> Support speaker notes[[fragment]]
* <span class="green">✓</span> Export to PDF[[fragment]]
* <span class="green">✓</span> Work offline[[fragment]]

<p class="fragment callout" style="border-left-color: var(--accent-green); background: rgba(166,227,161,0.06);">That's it. That's the whole requirements list.</p>

<aside class="notes">We've conflated "presentation software" with "the ability to present." PowerPoint does a thousand things. We need five. Why force AI to learn a thousand things when we only need five?</aside>
