## What if AI just…<br><span class="accent">built the app?</span>
<div style="margin-top: 36px;">
  <p class="fragment"><strong>HTML</strong> → content &amp; structure</p>
  <p class="fragment"><strong>CSS</strong> → styling &amp; animation</p>
